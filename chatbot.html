📖 How to Use GraphRAG Bot

🚀 Getting Started

GraphRAG Bot uses a multi-agent V4 pipeline — vector search, knowledge graph traversal, SQL, and AI reasoning — to answer questions about your documents.

🔒 Role-Based Access

  • Agent: Access to 100 documents
  • Manager: Access to 300 documents
  • Vice President: Access to all documents
  • When you upload a document, all roles above yours automatically gain access

🔍 Search Modes

  • General + Docs: AI knowledge combined with your documents
  • Only Documents: Answers drawn exclusively from your accessible documents

📚 Uploading Documents

  • Click Upload New Document in the sidebar
  • Enter your access key (assigned to your account)
  • Supported: PDF, DOCX, TXT, HTML, MD

💬 Chatting

  • Press Enter to send, Shift+Enter for new line
  • Every answer shows confidence and source citations
  • Conflicts between documents are surfaced automatically

🔍 KB Bot

The floating search panel (🔍 in the top bar) gives you quick one-off document queries without leaving your current view.
